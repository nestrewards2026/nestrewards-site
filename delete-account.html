Delete Account

NestRewards allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion by contacting:

support@nestrewards.in

What Happens After Request

Once a deletion request is received, your account and associated data will be scheduled for permanent deletion.

Data Deletion Timeline

Deleted account information and related data, including receipts and UPI IDs, are permanently removed within 90 days.

Important Notes

• You may be contacted for verification before processing the request.
• Any pending cashback or transactions may be reviewed before account deletion.
• Certain data may be retained if required for legal or compliance purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, please contact support@nestrewards.in.
